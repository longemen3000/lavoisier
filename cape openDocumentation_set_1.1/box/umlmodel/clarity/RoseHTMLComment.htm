Rose/HTML

Rose/HTML is a Rational Rose Add-In created by Clarity Development, LLC.  It is being offered free to the Rose community.  Since you get what you pay for, support is rather limited.  This web page contains an HTML form that, if you are connected to the Internet, will send us your comments, feature requests or bug reports. If you are not please visit our web site for contact information.

We are committed to making Rose/HTML a quality product.  Any and all feedback you can send is appreciated (and given the size of the Rose community, it might actually be read!)  This product was primarily created for our own benefit, and as such has only tested in environments that we work in.  If this product does not behave well in yours we want to know, because someday we might be there with you!

Please use this form to send us you thoughts.  All the information on this form is optional (although entering in nothing really doesn't do either of us much now does it).   We would appreciate your name and email address, and promise to not divulge it to anyone.  If you do enter your email address, we will in the future send you information on product enhancements and patches, however, at any time you may remove yourself from the list (we hate spam just as much as you).
