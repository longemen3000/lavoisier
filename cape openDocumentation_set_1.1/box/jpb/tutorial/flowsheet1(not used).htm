Flowsheet

 

 

This flowsheet Example is extracted from the Process Flowsheeting - A. Westerberg, H. Hutchison, R. Motard, P. Winter - Cambridge University Press - 1979.

 

The propylene feed is 2 mole % propane. In the reactor the propylene is being converted at 20 bar and 200°C to 2-hexene by the reaction :
2C3H6 --> C6H12

Conversion of 80% of the propylene is anticipated. The flash separator recovers unreacted propylene and much of the propane, which acts as an inert substance here. The bleed is to prevent too high a build-up of propane.
