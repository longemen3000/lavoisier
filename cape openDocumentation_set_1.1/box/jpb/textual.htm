SMST Specific Requirements

 

 

Coming from the analysis phase of the CAPE-OPEN Process, you can find below a synthesis from the main User Textual Requirements.

 

  • Within an unique CAPE-OPEN Sequential Modular Specific Tools (CO SMST) Component, the Partitioning functionality, the Ordering functionality, the Tearing functionality and the Sequencing functionality are integrated.
    They can be accessed separately and from different parts of the client.
    The Partitioning functionality and the Ordering functionality are merged into the Partitioning of the CO SMST Component.
    Therefore, this component will provide the services for Partitioning solver, Tearing solver and Sequencing solver.

  • In order to make easier the access to the all functionalities, the CO SMST Component can provide also a Full Sequential Modular Analysis solver.
    It makes the full Sequential Modular analysis : search of the calculation sequence of the partitions and units.
    So, that means it calculates the Partitioning of a flowsheet, next the Ordering of the partitions, next the Tearing of the partitions and next the Sequencing of units of each partitions.
    To propose the Full Sequential Modular Analysis solver, the CO SMST Component suppliers can reuse some/all already implemented solvers or can implement an independant solver. It is invisible to the client.

  • Through the CO SMST Component, the client can call services from each solver individually.
    The client can use one solver or several ones.

  • The CO SMST Component suppliers can provide one, several or all the four flowsheet solvers.
    The four flowsheet solvers are : Partitioning, Tearing, Sequencing and Full Sequential Modular Analysis.

  • The interface will be designed with the aim of transferring the graph connectivity and weightings.
    Therefore, the CO SMST Component doesn't need to have access to the complete unit and stream information. The units and streams of the flowsheet will be referenced by basic identifiers. So, no direct link between the CO SMST Component and a CO Unit (or Thermodynamic) Component is necessary.
    The graph connectivity represents the process flowsheet.
    The weightings calculated by the client contains the "process knowledge".
